Important Guidelines

Do's

  • Eat consistently – Never skip meals
  • Stay hydrated – 3-3.5L water daily (between meals)
  • Sleep 7-8 hours for muscle recovery & growth
  • Progressive overload – Gradually increase workout intensity
  • Track progress with weekly weigh-ins (same time)
  • Weekend testosterone-boosting meals are mandatory
  • Get 15-20 min morning sunlight for Vitamin D

Don'ts

  • Don't drink water during meals – Wait 30 min after
  • Don't skip post-workout nutrition – Critical window
  • Don't over-exercise – Rest is important for gains
  • Don't stress – Cortisol kills testosterone
  • Don't rely only on supplements – Real food is primary
  • Don't expect overnight results – Consistency wins
  • Don't compensate if you miss a meal – Just continue
